
#Logging#
-logs should be asynchronous
- must don't use printStackTrace ve System.out.println() because disrupts log structure (format(format can change in product or other steps))
-should be sensitive data (must don't use identity,email or special information)
-All logs should be collected in a central location and be in a specific format.
-Level should be used carefully.
-The different Levels and The different appends can be use

TRACE->DEBUG->INFO->WARN->ERROR->OFF

logging.pattern.console=%d{yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss} [%thread] %-5level %logger{36}
